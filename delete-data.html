Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. We will remove your login and anonymize your public identity. Games and statistics are retained for fairness and leaderboards, but are detached from personal identifiers.

Delete inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the IQ Chess app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Danger Zone.
  3. Tap Delete account, type DELETE, and confirm.

What happens:

  • Your sign-in (account) is deleted.
  • Your username becomes an anonymous handle (e.g., anonym_123456789).
  • Your public identity is anonymized; games and stats remain without personal identifiers.

Request deletion from the web (no app access)

If you can’t access the app, you can request deletion here:

Email request

We verify ownership and process your request by anonymizing your profile and removing login access.

Facebook Login users

You can also remove IQ Chess from your Facebook Apps and Websites settings. Facebook may display a confirmation code for your request.

What we keep vs. remove

  • Removed: your login credentials and personal identifiers we’re not legally required to keep.
  • Kept (anonymized): games, ratings, and stats needed for opponent history and integrity.
